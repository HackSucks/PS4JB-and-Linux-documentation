PS4 Linux Launcher

Before sending: kernel must match your southbridge. bzImage and initramfs.cpio.gz go on a FAT32 drive. The rootfs goes on an ext4 drive labeled psxitarch.
HDMI output will drop once the payload lands and the kernel starts booting. LED turning blue means it worked. Since this page can't read the server's response (see note below), that's your real confirmation — not the status box.
VRAM vs system RAM — PS4 has 8GB total. More VRAM = less left for Linux.
32MB: headless/server  ·  512MB–1GB: normal desktop use  ·  2GB: roughly what Orbis reserves  ·  3GB+: can get unstable
